The nested form is the selector and the update button. Hitting the update link performs the onsubmit (see RestaurantSelectForm.java) of the inner form, but does nothing for the outer form.
In this case, the inner hits accumulate until the outer is submitted, because of talking to the feedback panel Hitting the submit of the outer form does the inner and outer forms.
Name is a required field, but the validation for it will not be performed with the Update link.
