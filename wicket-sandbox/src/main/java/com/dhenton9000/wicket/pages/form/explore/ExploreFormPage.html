Exploring a cancel form

This form will have a cancel button that submits the form but bypasses validation. The idea is that all validators are contingent on what button is pressed. This means a complex form will require individual validations.

Also, the default validator that will not let 'fred' in for an integer in the backing bean will have to be bypassed by creating an holder that has all strings,which could be validated down stream

See: http://www.richardnichols.net/2010/10/implementing-a-draft-mode-with-apache-wicket-forms/
